A few words about me

My passions are a labirynth in which I'm forever lost, but without them I'm forever empty. There is so much that I adore and am always searching for a new fire within.
Half of my life, if not more, is spent in front of a screen. A screen is my work and my escape, my social life and a source for insparation. When I step away, I really do like how the wind feels on my skin. Those kind of moments are the ones that I remember and truly savour.
I believe in a harmony between body and spirit.
